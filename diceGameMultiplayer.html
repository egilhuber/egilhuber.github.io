How to Play

Gameplay

Player 1 begins the game.
Click 'roll dice' button to roll the dice.
Select one die each round to add to the scoring sheet. This die will be
eliminated from future rolls. Continue rolling the dice for 6 rounds, until
all the dice are eliminated. Then, click 'roll dice' to initiate the bonus
roll. Click 'roll dice' again
to initiate the final round. Then, click finish game.

Scoring

The objective of the game is to be the player with the highest score above 100.
In the bonus round, there is an opportunity to earn up to 20 extra points by
rolling the d20. In the final round, all 6 dice are rolled and
scored. In the case of a tie where both players have sub-100 scores, an additional
d20 roll is initiated by each player using their respective tie break button.
