How to Play

Gameplay

Begin gameplay by clicking "Begin Game". Click 'roll dice' button to roll the dice.
Select one die each round to add to the scoring sheet. This die will be
eliminated from future rolls. Continue rolling the dice for 6 rounds, until
all the dice are eliminated. Then, click 'roll dice' to initiate the bonus
roll, which gives you a randomly generated d20 score. Click 'roll dice' again
to initiate the final round, wherein all dice are rolled one final time
and added to the score sheet. Then, click finish game.

Scoring

The objective of the game is to score over 100.
In the bonus round, there is an opportunity to earn up to
20 extra points. In the final round, all 6 dice are rolled and
scored.
